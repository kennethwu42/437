Viewpoint

Prefumo Canyon

Offers gorgeous vistas accessible via turnouts along the road, featuring breathtaking rolling hills and perfect spots for sunsets.

Serenity Swing

Located in Poly Canyon Design Village, this swing provides expansive and unique views after a 2.5-mile hike.

Hearst Castle

Situated south of Big Sur, offering guided tours with jaw-dropping views of the Central Coast.

Cerro San Luis

Also known as Madonna Mountain, this moderate hike provides 360-degree views of San Luis Obispo from the summit.

Pirate’s Cove

A local favorite offering epic views from the road or beach, including a sea cave and pristine coastline vistas.

Terrace Hill

A short hike within city limits leading to a plateau with scenic city views, perfect for relaxation.

Islay Hill

A 1.8-mile round-trip hike offering sweeping views of the Santa Lucia Mountains and Edna Valley.

Cuesta Ridge Botanical Area

Starts from the top of Cuesta Grade, providing panoramic views early in the hike, including sights of eight of the Nine Sisters.

Point San Luis Lighthouse

Historic lighthouse nestled on the Pacific Ocean's edge, offering docent-led tours with stunning ocean views.

Bell Tower

A rigorous hike less than a mile to the top, rewarding with views of Cerro San Luis and a prominent bell tower.

Montaña de Oro

Popular destination with stunning views, expansive dunes, and the Bluff Trail offering coastal vistas.

Valencia Peak

One of the tallest mountains in Montaña de Oro, providing a 3.7-mile round-trip hike with 360-degree views at the summit.

Cerro Alto

At 2,624 feet, it's one of the highest peaks in SLO County, offering panoramic views of the entire Central Coast.

Ontario Ridge Trail

Known as “Shell Beach Ridge,” this trail runs between Shell Beach and Avila Beach, offering epic ocean views.

Broderson Peak Trail

Located within the Morro Dunes Ecological Reserve, this 2.5-mile hike includes a swing overlooking Morro Rock and the bay.
